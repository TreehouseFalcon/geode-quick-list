# Quick List

Trying to create large lists of levels that aren't easily searchable? Quick List can help!

All search results & saved levels have a new "quick-list" button that adds the level to your top created list. You may also configure the mod to open the "add to list" menu to select a different list.

Thanks to [imesss](user:36491794) ([ItMe12s](https://github.com/ItMe12s)) for creating the Luau API! You can check it out [here](https://github.com/ItMe12s/LuauAPI) to script your own mods easily :)
